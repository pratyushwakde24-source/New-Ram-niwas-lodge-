About Our Hotel

Elegant City Lodging For Modern Travelers

Situated at Part 1, Gandhi Sadan, 11, New Nagardas Road, next to the iconic Pinky Theater in Andheri East, New Ram Niwas Lodging House provides professional, trustworthy, and spotless accommodation for transit travelers, corporate executives, and families visiting Mumbai.

We are not an exaggerated 5-star resort; we take pride in being a reliable, high-comfort city hotel strategically located within walking distance of the Mumbai Metro and suburban rail network. Whether you are attending corporate sessions in MIDC/SEEPZ, catching an international flight, or exploring Mumbai's premier shopping and dining districts, our lodge offers the perfect peaceful sanctuary.

Metro & Transit Access

Walk just 350 meters (4 minutes) to Andheri Metro Station and 400 meters to Andheri Railway Station East.

15 Mins to Airport

Direct connectivity to Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport (BOM) Terminal 1 & 2 (~4.5 km).

Near Business Hubs

Quick 10-minute commute to MIDC IT Park, SEEPZ SEZ, NESCO Exhibition Centre, and Bandra Kurla Complex (BKC).

Dining & Shopping

Surrounded by popular restaurants, cafes, Phoenix Marketcity (~7km), and Andheri Market right outside.
